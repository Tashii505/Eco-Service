About Eco Service

Eco Service is a pollution-fighting, people-powered environmental organization in Bhutan.

We tackle plastic waste, water contamination, and air pollution through community action, eco-tech, and sustainable practices.

Our vision is a clean, healthy, and green Bhutan ‚Äî powered by informed citizens and supported by eco-friendly solutions.

Our Services

Recycling & Waste Management

We collect, sort, and recycle plastic, metal, and e-waste in urban and rural zones.

Water Purification

We install eco-friendly filtration systems in villages with unsafe water sources.

Air Quality Monitoring

Live air quality tracking with mobile units and community-based clean air drives.
